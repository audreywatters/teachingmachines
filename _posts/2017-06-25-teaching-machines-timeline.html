I’ve created a timeline for “The History of Teaching Machines” as part of my research for my book Teaching Machines. (I made this timeline in order to procrastinate revisiting my draft.)

Like “The History of Pearson” timeline I recently created, I’ve used timeline.js to build this site.

This timeline does not purport to offer a history of education technology. It’s a history of teaching machines, drawing mostly on the research I’ve published on Hack Education.

Consider this a work-in-progress. I’ll continue to fill in the gaps with missing details. (As this project is powered by a GitHub repository, you can always leave a message there or file an “issue” if you see something I’ve overlooked.)
