Teaching Machines: The Drive to Automate Education


Introduction


The Self-Driving Car and the MOOC. Sebastian Thrun. Big data. Efficiency. The drive to automate education.


Chapter 1: The Mechanics of Intelligence


The Matrix. Assessing human intelligence and building intelligent machines. IQ tests. Artificial intelligence and the Turing Test. Machine learning. Computers playing chess.


Chapter 2: Skinner's Teaching Machines


The history of ed-tech as knowledge delivery. B. F. Skinner. Behaviorism. Khan Academy.


Chapter 3: Robots, Labor, and Learning


Rossum's Universal Robots. Taylorism. Baumol's Cost Disease. Automated essay graders.


Chapter 4: Personalization, Standardization, and the Quantified Student


Intelligent tutoring systems. Adaptive learning. Analytics. Knewton. Who owns student data. Surveillance. Privacy and development of the self.


Conclusion


Ender's Game. Imperialism. MOOCs. Military's role in educational AI. Haraway's Cyborg Manifesto. Papert and the Children's Machine.

Author

Audrey Watters is an education writer, rabble-rouser, rambler, recovering academic, lifelong learner, serial dropout, part-time badass, mom.
